a bit about us

At Interlude Coffee, we believe every day deserves a pause. Inspired by the musical interlude - a short break between two parts of a story - we craft drinks that give you a moment to slow down, savor, and reset.

Founded by two friends who have worked in cafes across Vancouver. Interlude Coffee is a space to explore our full creative range as baristas. From familiar classics to new and unexpected flavour combinations, each drink is thoughtfully crafted to offer something a little different - and a short break in your day.
